How Emerald Works

Turning virtual success into real-world value

The Emerald Coin Ecosystem

Emerald Coin is a school-district-specific simulation platform designed to make financial education engaging and effective:

  • Virtual Stock & Crypto Exchange: Students can buy and trade in a risk-free environment that mimics real markets
  • Care Coin Rewards: Virtual gains convert to Care Coins—a real-value reward system used by participating schools
  • Curriculum Integration: Seamlessly complements courses like Microeconomics and Macroeconomics
  • School District Specific: Tailored to each district's needs and existing reward systems

Key Innovation: Unlike other simulators, Emerald ties student success to tangible outcomes, keeping learners motivated and truly invested in their financial education journey.

Our Technology Foundation

Powered by Supabase for security, scalability, and performance

Built on Supabase

Emerald Stock Exchange leverages Supabase, the open-source Firebase alternative, to deliver a secure, scalable, and seamless experience for schools and students.

Why Supabase?

We chose Supabase because it provides enterprise-grade features with the simplicity needed for educational environments:

  • Real-time Database: Instant updates to student portfolios and market data
  • Robust Authentication: Secure login and role-based access for students, teachers, and administrators
  • Automatic Scaling: Handles growth from single classrooms to entire districts seamlessly
  • Enhanced Security: Protects sensitive student data with enterprise-level security features
  • Cost-Effective: Allows us to keep the platform free for schools while maintaining high performance

With Supabase handling the backend complexity, we can focus on what matters most: creating an exceptional educational experience that prepares students for financial success.
